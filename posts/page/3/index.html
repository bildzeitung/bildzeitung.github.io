Advent of code: Day 7

There are lots of clever ways to approach Day 7 – a logic language (Prolog), a functional language that will just resolve the whole circuit by definition – that sort of thing. Since I’m currently a Python programmer, and this is a warm-up for the day, I opted for the brute force solution of passing over the data until every wire is resolved. It’s been pointed out to me that this is a very compiler-centric / parser kind of solution, and yes, that’s the way I thought of its.
