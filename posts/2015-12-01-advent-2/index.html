Advent of code: Day 1

December 1, 2015

The Day 1 puzzle is a parenthesis matching exercise, in a sense, but can be reduced down to a linear run through the input.

I managed to get the data for solving the first part of this challenge into a Unix one-liner:

{% highlight bash %} #!/bin/bash cat paren.in | perl -ne ‘print join(”\n”,split //);’ | sort | uniq -c {% endhighlight %}
